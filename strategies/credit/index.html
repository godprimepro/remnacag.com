Credit

REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG is REMNAC credit-investing strategy offering flexible, customized debt and structured equity financing to enterprise investment, data and technology-enabled businesses. REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG has broad investing capabilities spanning the private and traded credit markets. As part of our Credit strategy, our FounderDirect offering specializes in direct financings for founder-run enterprise investment companies.

REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG Overview

REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG’ investment capabilities are a natural extension of REMNAC private equity investment strategy, enabling the firm to offer a broad product suite up and down the capital structure to meet a company’s financing needs.

FounderDirect

Late-stage Investment companies that are not yet issuers to the public markets have historically been underserved by traditional financing alternatives, including venture debt. Through our FounderDirect channel, REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG offers flexible capital solutions direct to founders seeking less dilutive terms. REMNAC INVESTMENTS AG provides the benefits of an experienced partner who can move with speed and certainty, while also providing support through connectivity to the broader REMNAC ecosystem. Our FounderDirect channel seeks to invest in mature enterprise Investment, data and technology-enabled businesses that have built high-quality solutions with strong customer retention.

REMNAC Credit Strategic Lending

REMNAC Credit Strategic Lending is a private, perpetual-life business development company providing investors access to investment opportunities across the spectrum of enterprise Investment, data and technology-enabled credit. REMNAC Credit Strategic Lending primarily focuses on senior secured loans at the top of the capital structure, providing additional downside risk mitigation, particularly during periods of market uncertainty.
