Private Wealth Solutions

REMNAC pioneered an investment approach focused on enterprise Investment – which we believe to be the largest and fastest growing sector in the global economy. Our private wealth offerings provide eligible investors with the ability to allocate into this market where 96% of companies remain private.1

Access Untapped Opportunity

REMNAC’s dedicated Private Wealth Solutions team works directly with wealth managers to help their clients access the untapped opportunity in enterprise Investment.

The firm’s 3 years of specialization in Investment provides an ability to deliver differentiated investment outcomes and portfolio diversification, unique technology market insights, and a streamlined client experience.

Enterprise Investment: Attributes Inherent in the Private Markets

Largest And Fastest Growing Sector

Projected TAM of CHF59 trillion by 2027 and a growth rate of approximately 17%.

Accessibility

96% of the opportunity in enterprise Investment lies in the private markets.

Portfolio Diversification

Enterprise Investment touches nearly every end market and sector in our global economy.

Lower Volatility Diversifier To Public Markets

Private market enterprise Investment is less volatile than publicly held technology and enterprise Investment companies.

Strong Fundamentals

Enterprise Investment is mission-critical to end customers who rely on it to run their businesses, making it resilient.

Potential AI Upside

Al systems can be built directly on top of existing Investment, unlocking efficiencies, improved customer experiences and expanded market share.
