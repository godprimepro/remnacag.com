REMNAC Climate Pledge

We established the REMNAC Climate Pledge on Earth Day in 2023. The voluntary pledge consists of the following elements:

• Measuring GHG emissions
• Setting a GHG reduction target
• Reducing GHG emissions
• Offsetting GHG emissions

All majority owned private equity and permanent capital portfolio companies have access to a GHG measurement platform and best practice guidance for GHG emissions measurement and reductions.
